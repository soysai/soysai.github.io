Welcome to Diversify!

A big part of my language learning process is listening. Listening to music or watching shows helps make me more familiar to new vocabulary, and the catchy nature of songs allows me to recignize repeating words in the lyrics.

I have always had an inclination to listen to non-English music, but have relatively stuck to what I am familiar with - Japanese and Korean music. As an attempt to learn more languages and learn of all the beautiful songs in all the other languages, I decided to make weekly playlists with songs I was obsessed with the past week, actively seeking for songs in other langauges.

I hope you have a good listen!
